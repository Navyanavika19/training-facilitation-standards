# training-facilitation-standards
A structured collection of training facilitation standards, classroom operating guidelines, assessment practices, and feedback mechanisms designed to ensure professional, consistent, and outcome-driven learning delivery in instructor-led training programs.
## Training Program Guidelines & Classroom Operating Standards

This document outlines the standard operating guidelines followed during training delivery to ensure professionalism, consistency, learner engagement, and effective outcomes.

---

### 1. Professional Conduct & Etiquette

* All learners are required to attend training sessions in **formal attire** to maintain a professional learning environment.
* **English is the mandatory medium of communication** during training sessions to ensure uniform understanding and workplace readiness.
* **Punctuality is mandatory**. Learners are expected to join sessions on time and remain present for the full duration.

---

### 2. Session Structure & Time Management

* Training follows a **40-minute instruction session followed by a 10-minute break** to ensure optimal attention and retention.
* Each training day includes a balanced mix of **theoretical concepts and practical application**.
* Saturdays and Sundays are observed as **weekly holidays**.

---

### 3. Daily Recap & Knowledge Reinforcement

* A group of **5 learners per day** is assigned to deliver a **15-minute recap presentation** on the previous dayâ€™s topics before the new session begins.
* This activity reinforces learning, builds presentation skills, and encourages peer-to-peer knowledge sharing.

---

### 4. Self-Learning & Continuous Development

* Learners are expected to complete **daily self-learning courses or assignments** aligned with the training curriculum.
* Self-learning progress is monitored and discussed to encourage ownership of learning.

---

### 5. Engagement, Activities & Ownership

* Interactive activities are incorporated regularly to enhance engagement and participation.
* Learners are encouraged to **volunteer for tasks and responsibilities**, promoting confidence, accountability, and teamwork.

---

### 6. Attendance & Leave Policy

* Learners are entitled to **2 leaves per month**, subject to prior approval.
* Any additional absence may impact training continuity and performance evaluation.

---

### 7. Assessments, Mocks & Evaluation

* Regular **mock assessments** are conducted to evaluate understanding and skill application.
* Learners are **graded based on performance**, participation, and consistency.
* Assessment results are used to identify learning gaps and plan targeted improvement sessions.

---

### 8. Feedback & Continuous Improvement

* **Two-way feedback** is encouraged:

  * Trainers provide constructive feedback to learners.
  * Learners share feedback on training effectiveness and learning challenges.
* Feedback is reviewed and **actively implemented** to improve training delivery and learner outcomes.

---

### 9. Objective

The objective of these guidelines is to create a **disciplined, inclusive, and high-performance learning environment** that prepares learners for real-world professional roles through structured training, accountability, and continuous improvement.
